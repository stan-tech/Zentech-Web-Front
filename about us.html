At Zentech, the world is your workplace. We produce world-leading solutions for the household and commercial appliances segments, playing an important role in promoting well-being and positively impacting people’s daily lives.

Zentech manufactures and commercializes cooling solutions for a variety of refrigeration applications, as well as motors for dishwashers, washing machines, and dryers. Our focus is to deliver a comprehensive portfolio capable of meeting customer needs through high standards of quality, competitiveness, and energy efficiency.

In terms of compressors, the company has fixed-speed and variable-frequency products that meet the basic needs of household appliances worldwide and continues to make progress in environmental protection, high efficiency, small size, intelligence, and low noise. Its market share of air conditioning compressors ranks first globally, with annual sales exceeding 100 million units, and its market share of refrigerator compressors ranks among the top three globally.
